The basics

Most people are eligible to drive with us,

Here's what you need to know if you're driving in Nairobi or Mombasa.

1. Sign up online

Got an email address and a smartphone?

Great, now tell us a bit more about yourself and we'll get you started.

Sign up

2. Upload your documents

Time for some paperwork. There are some of the documents we need to see:

  • - National identity card
  • - Valid driving license
  • - Profile photo

3. Get assigned a vehicle

Make sure that your vehicle is in excellent working and physical condition,

so that it meets your and standards in Kenya.

4. Start driving today!

If you've completed all the above steps, congratulations!

You're ready to drive and start making money.

Your must haves:

  • - Motor vehicle registration book
  • - PSV insurance
  • - Vehicle inspection report
  • - Partner National ID

Vehicle inspections

Complete your vehicle inspection at your nearest inspection center.

Book your inspection

Find the nearest vehicle inspection location below and schedule an appointment.

The cost of vehicle inspection is KES 600 between 8:30 - 11AM on weekdays and KES 800 in any other operating hour.

Complete the Inspection

Complete your inspection on your booked time at the inspection center. There should be no damage indicated on the report.

Upload the inspection report

Once you pass, upload the clear / readable report to your account and ensure the vehicle registration number match the vehicle on your Uber profile. Uber does not cover repairs or maintenance to pass an inspection.
